   - Encourages starting small and mastering showing up

     * 22-66 days: Approaching breakthrough
     * 66+ days: Past breakthrough
   - Explanatory text:

     * "Results compound slowly, then suddenly. Keep going."

   - Automatic trigger every Sunday
   - Only shows if user has habits
   - Tracks last review date in localStorage
   - Shows once per week maximum
   - Beautiful modal with:
     * Large completion percentage

     * Personalized insights based on performance:

     * James Clear quote
   - Dismissible with "Got it, thanks!" button
   - Stores dismissal date to prevent spam
